---
title: Braised Turnips
subtitle: di Abruzzo
description: Excellent in the winter after the frosts
ingredients:
  - .5 cup olive oil
  - 1 garlic clove
  - 1 dried chili pepper
  - 1.5 lbs turnips, washed but no dried and cut into quarters
  - salt
directions: In a cast-iron skillet, heat the olive oil. Add the garlic, salt and
  chili pepper; stir, then add the turnips and stir to coat with the oil. Cook
  at slow heat with the skillet uncovered, stirring often to cook them evenly.
servings: .
author_name: Matt Rodigheri
